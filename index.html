PEATKEY RESERVE INFORMATION

In order to create the optimal ecological Reserve, the ecologists want to create a new distribution of Konik horses throughout the Peatkey Reserve.

Historically, Konik horse herds couldn’t migrate from one area of Peatkey Reserve to other areas
due to lakes that separated each area
. Ecologists have now created many animal crossings and noticed that apples are what attract horses to a specific area.

With this goal in mind, ecologists will plant new apple trees within the Peatkey Reserve in order to create a balanced amount of Konik horses throughout the
4 areas
. Aside from this, ecologists will also be placing new Konik horses into the Peatkey Reserve.

The ecologists have determined a very unlikely, potential, worst-case scenario in which adding apple trees will increase the original Konik horse (currently inhabiting Peatkey Reserve) apple consumption by
20%
. This possible scenario is isolated only to original Konik horses within
Langker Pasture and Revok Forest
.

If there are no apples available, new Konik horses (introduced in Y5) will eat carrots whereas the original Konik horses won’t. Carrots only grow in
Miley Pond and Elcobb Land
. Konik horses prefer apples over carrots, therefore,
3
carrots are necessary to feed/attract one new Konik horse. The ecologists have calculated that the amount of Konik horses within a herd differs depending on the area of the Peatkey Reserve.

  • Each of Elcobb Land's Konik horse herd has
    12
    horses
  • The ratio of # of Konik horses within a herd is the following:
    • Elcobb Land : Revok Forest : Miley Pond: Langker Pasture =
      3 : 5 : 8 : 7

The amount of apples on each apple tree depends on the area in which it is placed. The ecologists have calculated that
1 apple feeds and attracts 1 horse
and thus, it is possible to find the amount of apples that grow on each apple tree planted in a specific area by dividing the amount of Konik horses by the amount of apple trees. The number of apples per tree won’t change in year 5.

The ecologist team has currently defined the following activities to be completed at the end of year 4. Assume the growth of horses is possible only thanks to these activities.
  • For each 1 apple that is added, introduce 1 Konik horse into the Peatkey Reserve
  • Place 63 apple trees in Elcobb Land
  • Place 44 apple trees in Langker Pasture
  • Place 35 apple trees in Revok Forest
